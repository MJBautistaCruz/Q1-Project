CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
(Ibarra)
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Fiance of Maria Clara De Los Santos
  • Studied in Europe and was recently back to the Philippines after 7 years
Maria Clara De Los Santos
(Maria Clara)
  • Was born between Padre Damaso and Doña Pia Alba. When her mother died, Maria Clara was raised by Kaiptan Tiago.
  • Fiance of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin
  • Studied in a convent in Sta. Clara
Padre Damaso
  • The former town curate of San Diego
  • Real father of Maria Clara De Los Santos
  • The man who made all the efforts to keep Ibarra and Maria Clara drift apart for Maria Clara's sake
Kapitan Tiago
  • An influential businessman of San Diego
  • Husband of Doña Pia Alba and Maria Clara's supposed father
  • Betrothing his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra
Padre Salvi
  • A Spanish friar who arrives in the Philippines to serve in the Catholic Church
  • He discovered Padre Damaso's letters to Maria Clara's mother, Doña Pia, learning that Padre Damaso was in fact Maria Clara's real father.
  • He also conspired to kill Don Crisostomo Ibarra
